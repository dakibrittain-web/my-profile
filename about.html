About me

Frontend developer & textile artist from the Pacific Northwest

I'm a front-end developer working through SheCodes, with a background in Political Science, English Literature, and History. I bring a humanities thinkers eye to every project - clarity, structure, and the ability to translate complex ideas into plain language.

I specialise in accessible, thoughtfully designed interfaces — building for real users, not just screens.

Alongside coding, I run Wool and the Forest - a textile art business, rooted in slow craft, natural materials, and intentional making. My design sensibility is shaped by weaving & knitting: pattern, rhythm, structure, and the beauty of things made with care.

I'm based in the Netherlands, available for remote work internatiobnally, and looking for roles where design judgement and technical skill matter equally.
